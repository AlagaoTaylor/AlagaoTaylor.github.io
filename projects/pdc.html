Details

  • Name: Parallel & Distrubuted Computing (PDC) Quest Game Cards & Website
  • Date: December 2024 - May 2025
  • Located: Hawaii Pacific University

    • PDC Quest is a card game where each card has a question regarding PDC topics. The purpose is to encourage PDC topics in earlier stages of Computer Science programs.
    I was personally introduced to this game by the creator, my professor. Under a directed study, I got to learn more about Parallel & Distributed learning and the benefits of educating students of these topics earlier in the program. I was also given the great opportunity to design the cards and the intial website set up.
    The idea is that each card has a question, answer, and a QR code. The QR code navigates to the website that provides players with resources to learn more about the question & topic.

Skills Developed

  • HTML and CSS fundamentals/li>
  • Web & Graphic Design
  • Attention to detail
  • Version control

Reflections

Creating the playing cards and the website was a great way for me to learn more about PDC in a way. Understanding why things like load balancing and mutual exclusion are important as I read through the cards, the corresponding page, and found supporting resources.

And while my role in this initiative was quite small, I'm proud to have been able to contribute to such in any capacity that I could.
